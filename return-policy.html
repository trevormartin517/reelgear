Return Policy

All sales at ReelGearCo are final. We do not accept returns or exchanges on any of our products.

Each item is custom 3D printed to order, which means once production begins we are unable to restock or resell returned items. Because of this, we are unable to offer refunds for change of mind, incorrect model selection, or buyer's remorse.

We strongly encourage you to verify your Garmin model number before placing your order. If you are unsure which product fits your device, please reach out to us before purchasing and we will be happy to help.

In the rare event that your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with a photo of the issue and we will work to make it right.
